2.1 Logo Mark

The Kinvrs logo integrates the bold
`K' and dynamic shapes to create a
striking, modern design.

The geometric elements convey
strength and precision, while the
gradient flowing from dark purple
to vibrant purple symbolises
energy and transformation.

The angular forms reflect
innovation and forward movement,
making the logo an emblem of
creativity and progress.

The use of the purple gradient
adds a premium and futuristic feel,
perfectly aligning with Kinvrs'
vision of cutting-edge, impactful
technology.
Kinvrs Brand Manual                  Brand Identity  10

2.1 Logo Unit

The Kinvrs identity combines a
powerful symbol and modern
wordmark to establish a dynamic
and forward-thinking brand
presence.

The geometric "K" with a flowing
gradient reflects innovation,
energy, and progress, while the
sharp, bold lines of the wordmark
emphasise strength and clarity.

The sleek design symbolises Kinvrs'
cutting-edge approach to
technology and creativity.

Together, the logo and typeface
convey the brand's commitment to
modernity, precision, and
transformation, resonating with an
audience that values impactful,
visionary solutions.

3.3 Brand Gradient                     #040223        #4207c5

The Kinvrs brand gradient flows
from a deep, dark purple to a
vibrant, energetic purple hue,
symbolising a harmonious blend of
innovation, mystery, and creativity.
Starting with the deep and
grounded tone of Dark Purple
(#040223), it transitions into a bold
and dynamic shade of Purple
(#4207c5).

This gradient reflects the brand's
forward-thinking nature, with the
depth of the darker shade
symbolising strength and stability,
while the vibrant purple infuses a
sense of excitement and
modernity.

The smooth transition between
these shades creates a sense of
continuity and energy, making the
gradient perfect for backgrounds,
overlays, and transitions, ensuring
that the Kinvrs brand always feels
cutting-edge, confident, and
visually captivating.

5.1 Brand
Iconography

This is a modern and minimalist
icon set featuring clean, thin-line
designs.

It includes icons for media
controls, navigation,
communication, user profiles,
settings, connectivity, and file
management, making it ideal for
web and mobile apps.

The simple, uniform style ensures
easy integration into any digital
interface, providing intuitive visual
cues for user interactions.
Kinvrs Brand Manual                     Brand Visualisation  28

5.1 Brand
Imagery

The image combines futuristic and
digital themes, depicting a blend of
technology and human interaction.

It features vibrant, neon-lit
landscapes that suggest virtual
spaces or advanced technology,
alongside animated avatars that
hint at the rise of the metaverse.

A human figure engaged with
technology symbolises the
integration of digital tools into
daily life, while stylised, cybernetic
faces represent AI and virtual
reality.

The overall imagery reflects a
vision of innovation where artificial
intelligence, robotics, and digital
environments shape the future of
Kinvrs.
